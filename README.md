# UI-UX-Experiment2

## Aim:

Piano Master is a mobile music education app designed to help beginners and intermediate learners develop piano skills progressively — through structured lessons, interactive exercises, and real-time feedback — making piano learning accessible, engaging, and self-paced.

## Algorithm:
1.Signal Capture: Collects real-time audio from the device microphone at a 44.1 kHz sampling rate.
2.Noise Filtering: Applies a high-pass filter to remove background hums and environmental ambient noise.
3.Fast Fourier Transform (FFT): Converts the filtered time-domain audio signals into frequency-domain data blocks.
4.Fundamental Peak Detection: Identifies the strongest frequency spikes while filtering out overlapping harmonic overtones.
5.Frequency-to-Note Mapping: Matches the isolated fundamental frequency against standard MIDI pitch tables for instant validation.
